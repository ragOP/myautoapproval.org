FAQ
What people ask most before applying.
Does checking my rate affect my credit score?
No. We use a soft credit inquiry to show you rates — zero impact on your score. A hard inquiry only occurs when you formally accept an offer and give consent.
How quickly will I receive my funds?
Most approved borrowers receive funds the next business day after verification. Some banks support same-day ACH transfers. Timing varies by financial institution.
Are there any fees or prepayment penalties?
Zero origination fees. Zero prepayment penalties. Pay off your loan at any time with no extra charges. What you see is exactly what you pay.
What can I use the loan for?
Debt consolidation, home improvements, medical expenses, moving costs, weddings, major purchases — nearly anything. We do not fund illegal activities or market investments.
What are the eligibility requirements?
US resident, 18 or older, valid Social Security Number, active bank account, minimum $20K annual income. All credit profiles considered.
