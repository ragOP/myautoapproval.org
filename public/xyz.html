Questions
FAQ
The most common things people ask before applying.
Does checking my rate affect my credit score?
No. We use a soft credit inquiry to show you rates — this has zero impact on your score. A hard inquiry only occurs when you formally accept an offer and provide consent.
How quickly will I receive my funds?
Most approved borrowers receive funds the next business day after verification is complete. Some banks support same-day ACH. Timing may vary by your financial institution.
Are there any fees or prepayment penalties?
Zero origination fees. Zero prepayment penalties. You can pay off your loan at any time with no additional charges. What you see is exactly what you pay.
What can I use the loan for?
Debt consolidation, home improvements, medical expenses, moving costs, weddings, major purchases — almost anything. We do not fund illegal activities or stock market investments.
What are the basic eligibility requirements?
US resident, age 18+, valid Social Security Number, active checking or savings account, minimum annual income of $20,000. We consider all credit profiles.
